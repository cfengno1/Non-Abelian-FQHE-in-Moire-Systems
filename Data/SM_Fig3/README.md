sfig3_a.dat contains the single-particle band structures for the lowest 4 bands. 1st col is kx, 2nd col is ky, 3-6th cols are the band energies.
sfig3_bc.dat contains the second moire bands. 1st col is kx, 2nd col is ky, 3rd col is the band energy based on standard HF self energy, 4th col is based on empirical HF self energy.
